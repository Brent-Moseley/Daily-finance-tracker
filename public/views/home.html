Disclaimer:
Daily Financial Tracker web app is a no-charge tool for anyone to use to help keep track of and optimize their daily finances. Note that this is NOT a professional budgeting program, accounting package, or tax program. It is provided as-is for your free use. The intent of this app is to keep your data anonymous, letting you choose a user ID and a password. Please keep your data anonymous by NOT entering in any personally identifying information, such as full names, addresses, account numbers, etc. An export button is provided so you can backup your data to your local drive and / or import into excel or an accounting package (csv format). Backing up is highly recommended at least every month.
